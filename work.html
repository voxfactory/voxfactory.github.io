My Process

The process of getting your voxel is easy, simple, and quick.

Here's how it works!

First, you email me under the Order Voxel link. Include all of the details that you'd like so I can make my drafts as accurate as possible.

When I finish the drafts (allow 1-3 days) I will send you images of all of the voxel via email (the one you contacted me with).

Either you'll send back edit requests or be happy with the voxel. I will do the edits and send it back again.

Once you're happy, you'll pay me the requested amount either in Bitcoin (receive via email), PayPal* (email), or at gumroad.com/voxfactory * (buy at any time, custom orders will be uploaded).


* = Gumroad and PayPal will have extra charges because of the commision they take. It will be around $0.40.
