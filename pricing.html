Pricing - Save tons on great designs.

I have a pretty simple pricing system. It's cheaper than most professional designs (by a long shot) but will still get you a great result.

Small Design: 40x40x40 vox or less. $0.15 each

Medium Design: Between 40x40x40 and 60x60x60. $0.20 each

Large Design: Over 60x60x60. $0.25 for up to 70x70x70, and then + 5 cents for each one after. (e.g. 80x80x80 = $0.30)

You can also spice up your designs, with stuff like glass, metal, and light. Add $0.05 to each voxel with the effects.

You pay a starting price of $0.50 for the first seven models, then $0.40 for 8-10, $0.30 for 11-13, $0.20 for 14-16, and no starting charge for more than 16.
